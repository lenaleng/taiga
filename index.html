Welcome to Taiga Forest School

We are opening Taiga School because we believe in the power of nature-based education for children. In today's fast-paced world, an outdoor environment provides the perfect setting for kids to develop resilience, creativity, and a deep connection with the earth.

Curious about the benefits? We have collected compelling research on why forest preschool is so impactful for early childhood development. This approach perfectly aligns with our mission and values, which focus on child-led learning and fostering independence.

Our program is guided by passionate and experienced teachers who are dedicated to creating a safe, nurturing space for your child to explore. If you are ready to join our community, you can learn more about the admission process and review our tuition options to find the best fit for your family.
